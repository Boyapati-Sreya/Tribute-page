Tribute to Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao

About Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao

Full Name: Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao (popularly known as NTR)

Born: May 28, 1923, Nimmakuru, Andhra Pradesh, India

Died: January 18, 1996, Hyderabad, India

Profession(s): Actor, Filmmaker, Politician

Known As: “Viswa Vikhyatha Nata Sarvabhouma” (Universal Emperor of Acting)

Notable Roles: Lord Krishna, Lord Rama, Karna, Duryodhana, Ravana

Achievements

  • Cinematic Legend – Acted in over 300 films, especially known for mythological roles like Lord Krishna, Lord Rama, and Karna, earning the title “Nata Sarvabhouma”.
  • National Recognition – Awarded the Padma Shri (1968) and multiple Rashtrapati Awards for his exceptional contribution to Indian cinema.
  • Founder of Telugu Desam Party (1982) – Revolutionized Andhra Pradesh politics and gave Telugu people a strong political identity.
  • Three-time Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh – Introduced welfare schemes like ₹2 per kg rice and policies for women empowerment and rural development.
